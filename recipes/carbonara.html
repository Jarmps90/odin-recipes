Simple Pasta Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish combines a silky cheese sauce with crisp pancetta and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 400g
  • 15ml olive oil
  • 25 butter
  • 300g pancetta, sliced
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 50g grated pecorino
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Heat a large fry pan until hot, fry the pancetta in the oil until crispy, drain.
  2. In a bowl whisk the egg yolks, pecorino and parmesan. Season.

  3. Cook the fresh pasta in boiling salted water for 2 minutes. Drain and add to the bowl of egg mixture.

    Stir through, add the pancetta and a spoonful of pasta water and serve with more parmesan grated over the top.
